Get up and running with SV3 Player in just a few minutes.

## Installation

1. Download SV3 Player from the App Store
2. Open the app on your iPhone or iPad
3. Complete the brief onboarding flow

## Adding Your First Server

1. Go to **Settings** â†’ **Service Connections**
2. Tap **Add Service**
3. Select your server type (Stash, Plex, Emby, Jellyfin, or Custom GraphQL)
4. Enter your server details and authenticate
5. Tap **Test Connection** to verify
6. Save your configuration

## Next Steps

Once connected, you can:

- Browse your video library from the **Feed** view
- Use **Filters** to narrow down content
- Build a **Queue** of videos to watch
- Customize the app appearance in **Settings**

See the [Service Guides](/docs/services/) for detailed setup instructions for each server type.
